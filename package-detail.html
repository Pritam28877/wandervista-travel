Day-by-Day Itinerary

Viewing for: 21 Jun 2026 Change Date

Welcome to the start of your Spiti adventure! Arrive at Chandigarh Airport/Railway Station where our representative will greet you. Begin the scenic 7-hour drive to Shimla through winding mountain roads surrounded by pine forests. Check in to your hotel and enjoy the colonial charm of this hill station.

In the evening, take a leisurely stroll along the famous Mall Road and Ridge. Enjoy dinner at the hotel with panoramic valley views.

Dinner

After breakfast, drive from Shimla to Sarahan (175 km, ~7 hours). Stop at Narkanda for stunning views of the greater Himalayan range. The drive takes you through apple orchards and dense forests. Visit the magnificent Bhimakali Temple in Sarahan, an architectural marvel blending Hindu and Buddhist styles.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Drive to Chitkul - the last inhabited village near the Indo-Tibetan border. Pass through the stunning Sangla Valley with views of the Kinnaur Kailash range. The drive follows the beautiful Baspa River. Explore the charming village with its wooden houses and Buddhist temple.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Short drive to Kalpa (30 km) through spectacular mountain scenery. Kalpa offers some of the most dramatic views of the Kinnaur Kailash range with its signature shivling peak. Afternoon free to explore the village, visit the local monastery, and soak in the views of the sacred mountain.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Drive towards Tabo (210 km). Stop at Nako Lake - a sacred high-altitude lake surrounded by willow trees. Cross the dramatic Malling Nala stretch. Arrive at Tabo and visit the famous Tabo Monastery, often called the "Ajanta of the Himalayas" with murals dating back over 1,000 years.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Drive to Kaza, the administrative capital of Spiti (50 km). Stop at the dramatically perched Dhankar Monastery with stunning views of the confluence of Spiti and Pin Rivers. Explore the old Kaza town, visit the local market, and acclimatize to the altitude at 12,500 ft.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner

Full day exploration around Kaza! Visit the iconic Key Monastery - the largest in Spiti, perched dramatically on a hilltop. Drive up to Kibber village (14,200 ft) - one of the highest motorable villages. Visit Langza, home to the famous Buddha statue overlooking the valley. Hunt for million-year-old fossils in the rocky terrain.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner

The highlight of the trip! Drive towards Chandratal Lake (14,100 ft). Set up camp near the lake and take a 2 km trek to reach the mesmerizing crescent-shaped lake. The crystal-clear waters reflecting the surrounding peaks create an ethereal experience. Evening bonfire and stargazing at one of India's clearest night skies.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner Evening Snacks

Epic drive from Chandratal to Manali crossing the Kunzum Pass (15,060 ft) and descending through the Atal Tunnel (world's highest highway tunnel at 10,000 ft). The drive through Lahaul Valley offers dramatic landscapes. Arrive in Manali by evening and enjoy the riverside town vibes.

Breakfast Lunch Dinner

A well-deserved leisure day in Manali! Visit Hadimba Temple, Old Manali, Vashisht Hot Springs, and Mall Road. Optional activities include river rafting on the Beas, paragliding at Solang Valley, or just relaxing at a riverside cafe. Evening free for shopping.

Breakfast Dinner

Drive back to Chandigarh (310 km, ~9 hours) through scenic Kullu Valley, Sundernagar, and Bilaspur. Stop at Pandoh Dam for photo opportunities. Arrive in Chandigarh by evening. Farewell dinner with the group - share memories from the incredible journey.

Breakfast Dinner

After breakfast, check out and transfer to Chandigarh Airport/Railway Station. Your epic Spiti Valley adventure concludes here with a heart full of memories and a camera full of photos. Until next time, keep wandering!
