Our Story

From Backpackers to India's Trusted Travel Partner

In 2014, Arjun Mehta and Kavita Sharma set out on a road trip to Spiti Valley with nothing but a borrowed car and a crumpled map. That trip changed everything. They realized that millions of Indians wanted to explore their own country but lacked reliable, well-organized travel options.

WanderVista was born from that insight. We started with just 3 Himachal Pradesh packages and today offer 500+ curated journeys across India and 15 international destinations. Every package is personally tested by our team before we offer it to you.

Our philosophy is simple: travel should be transformative, not transactional. We don't just book hotels and buses - we craft experiences that change how you see the world.
