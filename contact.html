Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive

You can book directly on our website by clicking "Book Now" on any package page. Alternatively, you can call us at 1800-123-4567, WhatsApp us, or fill out the enquiry form above. Our travel expert will guide you through the entire process, help you choose dates, and finalize your booking.

Our cancellation policy varies by package but generally follows a tiered structure: 10% deduction for cancellations 121+ days before departure, scaling up to 100% for cancellations within 15 days. Full details are available on each package page under the "Cancellation Policy" tab. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days.

Yes! All our domestic and international packages include complimentary travel insurance for travelers up to age 70. The insurance covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation due to unforeseen circumstances, and baggage loss. For travelers above 70, additional insurance can be arranged at a nominal cost.

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable packages. You can modify the duration, hotels, activities, and even create an entirely new itinerary. Fill out the contact form above with your requirements, or call us, and our travel planning team will create a personalized package within 24 hours.

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets. We also offer: (1) Full payment upfront, (2) 30% advance with remaining due 30 days before departure, and (3) No-cost EMI options starting at ₹2,999/month on select bank cards. All payments are processed through secure, PCI-DSS compliant gateways.

Safety is our top priority. We have specific Women's Special tours designed exclusively for female travelers, led by experienced female tour managers. All our tours maintain strict safety protocols, 24/7 emergency support, and vetted accommodations. Over 35% of our solo travelers are women, and we have a 4.9/5 safety rating from female travelers.

In case of force majeure events (extreme weather, natural disasters, government restrictions), we offer two options: (1) Full credit for a future trip of equal or higher value, valid for 12 months, or (2) Refund of the package cost minus non-recoverable expenses (like already-issued permits or pre-booked flights). We always try to offer alternative dates or routes when possible.
